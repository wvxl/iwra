Our Mission and Vision

Recycle -> Redesign -> Reapply

IWRA aims to be the leading platform and community for researchers, educators, policy influencers, and managers who are addressing local to global wood-related challenges. We are dedicated to improve and provide customised service of the re-usage of wood outcomes through sustainable management practices. IWRA is to serve as a cross-disciplinary, membership-focused association that facilitates and inspires dialogue, knowledge sharing, and science-based solutions for the sustainable management of wood resources across various sectors and communities. Ranging from promoting education, advancing research, and enhancing decision-making, we aspire to become the pinoeer of wood recycling in the industry.
